Usage

Sit upright

Ensure no restrictions to chest.

Breathe normally

Inhale and exhale normally for a few breaths.

Blow

Blow as hard and as fast as possible into Spiroflow until no more air comes out.

What Spiroflow Measures

FEV1 Forced Expiratory Volume

Volume exhaled in one second

FVC Forced Vital Capacity

Volume exhaled in one complete breath

T Temperature

R Relative Humidity

Environmental parameters that affect your lung

Spirometry and Diagnosis

Obstructive

Asthma, COPD

FEV1 reduced, FVC constant, FEV1/FVC ratio reduced

Restrictive

Tuberculosis, Pneumoconiosis

FVC reduced more than FEV1, FEV1/FVC ratio increased

Disease FVC FEV1 FEV1/FVC ratio
Obstructive Normal Low Low
Restrictive Low Lower Normal
Combined Low Low Low
